## 创作理念

"《你是我的小狗狗》这首歌是根据我自己养的小狗狗来写的，可能有知道的粉丝知道，我养了两只小狗狗，一只叫“歪宝”是我第一次养的狗，黑白边牧，还有一只是“C宝”，是一只陨石边牧。这两只狗的性格不一样，都很可爱，治愈了我很多，给我平时生活中带来了很多很多的精神支柱。"

## Statement

"“You Are My Dog” was actually written based on my own dogs. Some fans might already know that I have two: one is Viper, my first dog, a black-and-white Border Collie; the other is Saber, a merle Border Collie. Their personalities are very different, but both are incredibly adorable. They’ve healed me in so many ways and have become an enormous emotional support in my daily life."
