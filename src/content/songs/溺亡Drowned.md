## 创作理念

"很多时候，人会不小心去迎合别人，会失去自己，可能会在纠结中不断地杀shi自己，把自己溺亡的那种感觉，就是无法呼吸，一种纠结的感觉"

“身边的人告诉我：我付出的每一份善意，都会被标上明确的价格。我觉得他们太计较每一份得失。可我，却因为害怕失去，依然去讨好他人。不管是谁的错，最后都归结于自己。情绪就像是一道枷锁，将我包裹并将我杀死。当有一天，冷漠代替善意，我还是不是自己？”


## Statement


"A lot of the time, people unconsciously start molding themselves to please others, and little by little, they lose who they are. You end up constantly tearing yourself apart in the middle of all that conflict—like drowning yourself emotionally, unable to breathe, trapped in that suffocating feeling of inner struggle."

"People around me told me that every bit of kindness I give will eventually be assigned a clear price. I thought they were too calculating about every gain and loss. But I, out of fear of losing people, still kept trying to please others. No matter whose fault it was, I always ended up blaming myself. Emotions became like shackles—wrapping around me and slowly destroying me. And one day, if indifference replaces kindness… will I still be myself?"
