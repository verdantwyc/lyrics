## 创作理念

"《握不住的光》这首歌呢是我的“十周年曲”，是我写给我自己的十周年偶像生涯的一个感受。然后“至暗时刻”的话，总之我希望之前所有的不愉快经历，全都是“至暗时刻”，以后的路全都是光明的。"


## Statement

" “The Light I Can’t Hold” is my “10th Anniversary Song,” written as a reflection on my decade-long journey as an idol. As for the “darkest moments,” I just hope that all past hardships remain as those darkest moments, and that the road ahead is filled entirely with light."
