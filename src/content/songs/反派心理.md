## 创作理念

"很多影视作品很多比较出彩的反派角色了，然后就会有那个反派角色，是我的上一个专辑的歌曲。这个专辑的《反派心理》也是“反派系列”的第二首歌曲，然后我觉得道德伪装是给恶行“贴金箔”。比如说最近的网络审判者，很多都打着正义的旗号来施暴，人们就是处于那种恶的不纯粹，善的也不纯粹的那种临界点。我觉得守住自己的底线，否则有一天会被自己的回旋镖打到。"

## Statement

"Actually, many film and television works already feature quite remarkable villain characters. In fact, I wrote a song about a villain in my previous album, and “Villain’s Psyche” is actually the second song in this “villain series.” To me, “moral disguise” means gilding wrongdoing with a layer of righteousness, giving evil a golden mask. For example, many so-called “online judges” today commit acts of violence under the banner of justice. We’re now living at this fragile boundary where neither good nor evil is pure. I think the key is to hold onto your own moral bottom line; otherwise, one day, your own boomerang might come back and hit you."
