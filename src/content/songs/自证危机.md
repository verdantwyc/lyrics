## 创作理念

内容摘自咪咕视频《音乐新势力》Vol. 161

"其实像我这个职业很容易就陷入“自证危机”，因为一句话通过很多人的嘴传过去之后就会变味了，然后人们都会只想听到自己想听到的事实，就是所谓的事实啊，然后一旦你做的事情跟他们想的不一样，他们就会疯狂地攻击你，然后任何像我们这种作为公众人物任何事情都会放大，所以容易陷入自证危机。然后我这首歌呢其实是用比较戏谑的语气，唱出人生有限，不要跟“傻子”斗嘴，其实是用这个经验来换来的觉悟，也是对自己说的话。 "

## Statement

"In my line of work, it’s actually very easy to fall into this kind of crisis. When a single sentence gets passed through many mouths, its meaning changes, and people only want to hear what confirms their version of the “truth.” Once what you do doesn’t match their expectations, they start attacking you relentlessly. For public figures like us, everything gets magnified, so it’s easy to end up trapped in this cycle. This song uses a somewhat playful tone to say that life is short. Don’t waste it arguing with fools. It’s a lesson learned through experience, and really, it’s something I’m also reminding myself of."
