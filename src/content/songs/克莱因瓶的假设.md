## 创作理念

"其实用物理学概念写歌是来自理科生的浪漫。克莱因瓶是两个莫比乌斯环结合产生的一个是一种假设，它是不分内外的。很多人用它来比喻，就是爱情。"


## Statement

"Using physics concepts in songwriting comes from the kind of romance only a science student would have. The Klein bottle is formed by joining two Möbius strips together. It’s a theoretical object that has no distinction between inside and outside. Many people use it as a metaphor for love."
