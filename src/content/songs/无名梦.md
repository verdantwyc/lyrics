## Lyrics Translation

Jiang Yun:

Bare crows perch on dead autumn boughs
Slant dusk falls on lone geese back home

Back then you and I were like leaves holding dew
Still believed brief goodbyes meant we’d meet somewhere soon

Road we came overgrown with weeds
Swallows cross through courtyard eaves
Black tiles white walls stay the same
Yet people changed

Moonlight falls lake clouds drift loose
Days once lived  won’t return anew
Like the moon upon dark water blue
Beyond our touch


Both：

Nothing here in this world can stay
Beauty leaves mirrors flowers fade away
Longing beneath lamp light left with none to say
Only blame farewell came too fast 

Fleeting lights and shadows people rush along
Youthful days remembered scatter with the wind
Like a dream from another life now gone
