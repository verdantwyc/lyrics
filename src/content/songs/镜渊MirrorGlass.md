## 创作理念

创作理念摘自咪咕视频《音乐新势力》Vol. 16。

"这首歌是我自己作词作曲的原创曲目，以镜子和光为元素，表达了自我人格间的挣扎。 "

"因为我平时都很喜欢思考一些奇奇怪怪的问题，然后镜子呢，是一个很神奇的东西，我有时候安静的时候会对着镜子思考。我想镜中的自己是不是也会有自己的思想，然后镜子里面的反射是由光造成的，光其实是主谋。我就会觉得镜子跟光这两个元素是非常非常有意思的东西，就会拿来思考很多很多。"

"其实这个是源自于我的某些时刻的梦境，比如说“左眼结出冰凌的痂”还有“右瞳染出硫磺的疤”就是“右瞳燃烧”，就是其实是一个很“中二”的画面感，就非常喜欢这种画面感，但是那个梦境因为无法描述就只能用歌曲来记录下来。"

## Statement

"This is an original song written and composed by myself. It takes mirrors and light as its core symbols, exploring the inner struggle between different sides of one’s self. "

"Well, I’ve always liked thinking about odd, abstract questions. The mirror is such a fascinating object. Sometimes, when I’m quiet, I just find myself staring into it and wondering: does the person in the reflection have thoughts of their own? Then I think about how that reflection exists because of light. Light is the real culprit, the mastermind behind what we see. I just think that the mirror and the light are both really, really interesting things, so I often take them to think about a lot, a lot of things. "

"Actually, it came from some of my dreams at certain moments. For example, lines like “Left eye crusted with ice and scar” and “Right eye burns like a sulfur star” — the right pupil burning — these are quite “chuunibyou” kinds of images. I really love that kind of visual intensity. Since those dreams were indescribable in words, I could only record them through the song. "
