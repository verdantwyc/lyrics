## Lyrics Translation


In tangled fantasies, she finds her soul
Opens the door to a reality she can't return to whole
She is like a cold autumn clear
Waiting for the night wind to dry each tear

She calmly says goodbye to days gone by
Beyond the spotlight, her story keeps writing
Turns and walks through lonely rainy nights
Black roses sway in sight

She writes life's joys and sorrows into a script
All living crowds become dust adrift
Life itself is only theater's art
Love, hate, and youth blaze through the heart

She seeks answers in the star chart's light
To all the world has given her in sight
She calmly says goodbye to time
Lonely, yet burning bright
Turns and cuts the chains of pain
Red roses shine again
