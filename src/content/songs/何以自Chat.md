## 创作理念

"灵感来自ChatGPT。不是有人说可以用AI写词嘛？作曲、作画，代替人工。谐音何以自洽。写是能写，没得灵魂"

"《何以自Chat》那首歌之前好像有说过，关于AI的故事。"

## Statement

"The inspiration for this song came from ChatGPT. Didn't some say that AI can be used to write lyrics? For composing, drawing, and replacing manual work. The song title is a play on the words “how to be self-consistent”. While AI can write, it lacks soul."

"It seems that I've mentioned about the song “How to Self-Converse” before; it's about a story related to AI."
