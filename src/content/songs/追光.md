## 创作理念

### 首次演出时的说明（20-10-17生日公演）


可能我粉丝知道我最近有自己写了一首歌，因为我第一次写歌，这首歌还很毛坯，没有写过歌，很幼稚。
 
我是想着你们的样子写的，就可能你们也很幼稚。尝试写了一下，没有学过音乐嘛[^1]，很想尝试这种感觉，希望大家⋯也不是说大家可以喜欢吧，就大家听一听就好啦。

 
[^1]:当时天草刚买了琴，不过生日公演时用的是前司的琴

### 直播的说明/分享（22-10-30生日公演）


我好像很久之前说想原创歌曲，但一直都没有尝试过，然后这首歌就是我的第一首原创歌曲。当时候生日公演唱了。然后，大家居然那么的喜欢，就让我有勇气写更多的原创歌曲。

虽然说我自己个人觉得，目前来说比较幼稚，或者说比较没有那么成熟，但就是说大家会支持我，就会想要，就是⋯嗯，对，更加多的去创作。

其实还是挺想有那种专业团队，就是帮我写自己的原创歌曲的。但是有时候就是比较难嘛，所以就是一步步自己来摸索。希望之后能够自己去学一下编曲吧。

## Statement

### Remarks during the debut performance(20-10-17, Birthday Performance)

Some of my fans might already know that I recently ventured into songwriting. Being my first attempt, the song is quite rough around the edges and may come off as a bit naive.

The inspiration behind the song was all of you, thinking perhaps it mirrors the innocence we share. While I’ve never been formally trained in music[^1], I wanted to give songwriting a shot. It’s not so much about everyone loving it, but more about hoping you’d take a moment to listen.

 

[^1]:SkyGrass got a new piano then, but the one used during the birthday performance was from her previous company.


### Sharing during the live steam(22-10-30 live stream)

I mentioned quite some time ago that I wanted to create songs, but I never got around to it until then. This song is my very first original piece. I performed it during my birthday performance, and to my surprise, everyone seemed to love it. It's given me the courage to write even more original songs.

Personally, I feel it might come off as a bit naive or perhaps not as polished as I'd like. But knowing that you all support me encourages me to dive deeper into songwriting.

I do wish I had a professional team to help me craft my songs. However, finding the right fit can be challenging, so I'm taking it one step at a time, learning as I go. Hopefully, I can pick up some skills in music arrangement in the future.
