## Lyrics Translation

I wish I could tell you—I’m not that happy
Smiling in the corner, but my heart feels heavy
I refuse to be the victim they’ve designed
Carrying scars too deep to leave behind
 

We once walked through winter and summer skies
Setting out time after time
If your joy became my joy
It was because of you—always you
 

Can we make it
Through brilliance, right up to gray-haired years?
Will we be met
With thorns or blooming flowers here?


In the blink of an eye, in a fleeting instant, it’s still you
We make a wish, take off, and race toward our truth
I remember that day—launching paper planes
Laughing or crying, so lucky to have you by my side again
Dadadadada.
